Planet Nine

Daily Tracker — News & Probable Location

Status

Planet Nine is a hypothetical planet in the outer solar system, proposed by Konstantin Batygin and Mike Brown (Caltech, 2016) to explain the clustered orbits of distant trans-Neptunian objects. It has not been directly observed.

  • Estimated mass5–10 Earth masses
  • Semi-major axis~400–800 AU
  • Orbital period~10,000–20,000 years
  • Eccentricity~0.2–0.5
  • Inclination~15–25°
